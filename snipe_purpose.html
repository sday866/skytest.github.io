Snipe Purpose

To create a thrilling, community-driven photo-sniping game that blends creativity, stealth, and social interaction into an immersive and versatile experience.

Our startup is focused on creating an engaging social experience through a photo-based game where players capture stealthy photos of each other. Snipebot promotes active connections among players, communities, and organizations by allowing users to competitively interact with one another. With the versatility to adapt to various environments—from urban landscapes to campus settings—our game fosters a vibrant, inclusive community. The inclusion of features like time-bound re-entry, group play, and real-time reactions enriches the user experience, making Snipebot unique and thrilling. That said, we are committed to providing a safe, fun, and competitive environment where spontaneity and community spirit are at the heart of every interaction.

Snipebot is a photo-based game that enhances social connections by allowing players to capture stealthy photos in various settings, from urban landscapes to campuses, creating a vibrant, inclusive community.
